Submission


Submission method

We welcome full-length paper submissions of maximum 12 pages in the CCIS one-column page format. Short papers of at most 8 pages will also be considered. Submissions should strictly adhere to the Springer Communications in Computer and Information Science (CCIS) format. Submissions will be accepted through Springer Online Conference Service (OCS).

Academic ethics

Please do not submit work that has been already published or accepted for publication or is currently under review for other conferences or journals. All manuscripts submitted to TEAMC 2018 will be subjected to plagiarism detection software to ensure originality. Qualifying papers will undergo a double blind review process by at least three reviewers. Accepted papers must be presented at the conference.

Paper preparation guidelines

Please ensure that your manuscript clearly brings forth the innovative ideas in it, the results obtained and relevance of the contribution.

Submit the title page containing the authors details.

Separately submit the manuscript with no author details

See the instructions for Double-Blind Peer Review here

Registration requirement 

At least one of the authors of each accepted paper must register by paying the full registration fee as applicable for the author, on or before the deadline for camera ready submission.  

Awards

Best Paper awards will be presented to high quality papers. 

Publishing

We are in active engagement with Springer for publishing accepted papers of the conference in the CCIS book series, subject to approval by CCIS Board. The publication will be indexed by ISI Proceedings, Ei – Compendex,  DBLP,  SCOPUS, Google Scholar and Springer link.

We are also contacting other publishers for journal publications and will share emerging developments on this website.
